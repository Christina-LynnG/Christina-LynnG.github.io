Pine

Conifer

Pine trees are considered evergreens because they keep their needles for approximately 2 years. When old needles falls, new needles quickly take their place. Pine tree needles can range in length from 1 inch to 11 inches. Both male and female pine trees produce woody cones

Oak

Deciduous

Oak belongs to the genus Quercus which comprises over 600 different species of trees. Most species of oak trees are deciduous with only couple of evergreen forms. Oak trees live on the Northern hemisphere.They can survive in various forests

Maple

Acer

While most maples are trees that can reach anywhere from 10 metres to 45 metres tall, other maples are shrubs of less than 10 metres in height. Leaves of maples are usually deciduous in opposite pairs and are palmately veined and lobed in most species
